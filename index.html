Office Hours Redesigned
Liz Merrigan, Rachel Halpern, Han Wen Zhang, Amita Shukla
In Spring 2020, Zoom office hours presented both challenges and opportunities for many students. When we return to campus, office hours will need to be socially distanced. We wanted to create a new model for office hours that keeps the usefulness and interpersonal connections of the old in-person model while also being time-efficient for students and professors.
How might we improve the office hours experience?
We conducted research with 30 Columbia and Barnard students across different departments to learn about their office hours experiences.
How might we create a tool for students to organize office hours?
Based on our research, we created a set of mockups. See the clickable Figma prototype here.
